About Calorie Calculator

Built with ❤️ for anyone serious about their health

Calorie Calculator is a free, no-signup calorie and macro calculator designed for people who want clear, science-backed answers — without the clutter of apps that require creating accounts.

How it works

We use the Mifflin–St Jeor equation — the most validated BMR formula for modern adults — combined with standard activity multipliers to estimate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). Your calorie target is then adjusted based on your goal.

Macro splits are goal-specific: higher protein is recommended during a calorie deficit to preserve muscle mass, while moderate splits are used for maintenance and lean bulking phases.

Our mission

Nutrition tracking doesn't need to be complicated. This calculator gives you the numbers you need in under 30 seconds. No required accounts, no subscriptions — just useful information.

The 30 Day Discipline Reset PDFs

Want to build habits that actually stick? We offer two versions of our 30 Day Discipline Reset PDF — both with daily habit tick boxes, a notes section for each day, and reflection prompts:

  • Starter Edition — beginner-friendly, gentle prompts, easy wins to build momentum from day one.
